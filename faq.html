Complete the registration form on our website and the relevant team manager will contact you with next steps. If the age group is full, you'll be added to a waiting list. We serve Steyning, Bramber, Henfield, Upper Beeding, Washington, Storrington and surrounding West Sussex villages. Email steyningtownfc@gmail.com with any questions.

The Men's First Team compete in the Pitching-In Isthmian League South East Division for 2026-27, after winning the SCFL Premier Division title in 2025-26. The Women's First Team play in the London & South East Regional Women's Football League (Division One South). The club also has teams in the Horsham & District Youth FL, Sussex County Women & Girls FL, and Sussex Sunday Football League.

Home games are played at the Peter Humphreys Memorial Ground, Shooting Field, Steyning, West Sussex, BN44 3RQ. The ground is accessible from the A283, with free parking on site. The facility features a £450,000 3G all-weather pitch (FA & FIFA accredited) and a refurbished clubhouse with bar.

Men's First Team: Adults £10 · Concessions £6 · Under 16s free. Women's First Team: £3 for all · Under 16s and concessions free. Free parking is available at Shooting Field.

The Development Centre offers after-school football coaching for boys and girls aged 3–15 on the 3G pitch at Shooting Field. Sessions run Monday–Friday from 3:45–4:45pm and cost £10 per session. They're led by qualified coaches including the first team manager. Individual 1-to-1 coaching is also available. Open to players already registered with the club and newcomers alike.

The Clubhouse can be booked for private parties, corporate events, anniversaries, live music, and children's football parties (with 3G pitch + party tea). Email theclubhouse.steyning@gmail.com to enquire.

Every registered player receives a full playing strip (shirt, shorts and socks) — the kit remains club property. Annual fees for 2025-26 are: Mini-Kickers to U7 £200 · U8–U10 £280 · U11–U12 £300 · U13–U18 £320. Girls' fees are lower to encourage participation. Financial assistance is available — contact the chairman or welfare officer in confidence.

Steyning Town FC was founded in 1892 as simply "Steyning" and became a founding member of the West Sussex Football League in 1896. The club became Steyning Town Community FC in 2013 after merging with Steyning Strikers Juniors, growing to 18+ teams at the time. Today the club runs 30+ teams with 500+ registered players and holds ★★ England Football accreditation.

The 2026–27 away kit is blue and black, produced by Edge Teamwear. The home kit features the club's traditional red and white vertical stripes. For kit orders or sizing enquiries, email steyningtownfc@gmail.com.
