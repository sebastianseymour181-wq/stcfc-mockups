Development Centre

The Development Centre offers after-school football coaching for boys and girls aged 3–15 at Shooting Field. Sessions run Monday–Friday, 3:45–4:45pm and cost £10 per session. Led by qualified coaches including the first team manager. Individual 1-to-1 coaching also available. Open to all — no need to be registered with the club.
