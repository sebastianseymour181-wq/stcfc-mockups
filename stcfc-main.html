Our History

1892
Club Founded
Established as "Steyning" — one of Sussex's oldest football clubs.
1896
West Sussex League
Became a founding member of the West Sussex Football League.
1979
Renamed
Officially became Steyning Town Football Club.
1984–86
Golden Era
Won Division One title, the Sussex Senior Cup twice and reached the FA Vase quarter-finals.
2013
Community Club
Merged with Steyning Strikers to become Steyning Town Community FC, growing to 18+ teams.
2015
3G Pitch Opened
£450,000 all-weather pitch at Shooting Field — funded by the Wilson Trust, HSBC and The Hub.
2016–17
Club of the Year
Won the Sussex FA Community Club of the Year award back-to-back.
2018
Women's Team
Established the Women's First Team, now competing in the London & South East Regional WFL.
2023–24
First Promotion
SCFL Premier champions and Peter Bentley Cup winners. First ever Isthmian League promotion.
2025–26
Champions Again
Returned to and won the SCFL Premier for a second time. Back in the Isthmian League.
