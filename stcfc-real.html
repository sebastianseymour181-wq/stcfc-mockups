A History Of
Success

🏆
SCFL Premier Champions
2023–24 & 2025–26 — Back-to-back title wins secured promotion to the Isthmian League.
🏅
Sussex Senior Cup Winners
1985–86 & 1988–89. Also Sussex County League Division One champions in 1984–85 and 1985–86.
⭐
Community Club of the Year
Sussex FA Community Club of the Year 2016 & 2017. 2-Star England Football accreditation holders.
👩‍⚽
Women's Back-to-Back Titles
SECWFL Division One West champions and League Cup winners in 2022–23.

FA Vase Quarter-Finals
Reached the last eight of the FA Vase in 1984–85 — the furthest the club has ever gone in the competition.

3G Pitch — Shooting Field
£450,000 state-of-the-art 3G pitch opened in 2015. One of the best facilities in non-league Sussex.
