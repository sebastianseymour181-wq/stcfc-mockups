Steyning Town Community FC are proud to announce acceptance into the **Isthmian U18s League** for the 2026–27 season — a first in the club's 134-year history.

This represents a significant step forward for youth development at the club, providing our most talented young players with a higher standard of football to develop in. The U18s will compete alongside the men's first team in Isthmian League football next season.

The news adds to an exciting summer for the club, which is also returning to Step 4 football for the second time under new manager Brannon O'Neill.

More details on U18s trials and pre-season arrangements will be announced shortly.
