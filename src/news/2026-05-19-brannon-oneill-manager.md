Steyning Town Community FC are delighted to announce the appointment of Brannon O'Neill as Men's First Team Manager ahead of the 2026–27 Pitching-In Isthmian League South East Division campaign.

O'Neill brings a wealth of experience to the role, with over 740 appearances in non-league football. He takes charge as the club prepares for their return to Step 4 football following back-to-back SCFL Premier Division title wins.

The new manager will be looking to build on the foundations laid in recent seasons and put his own stamp on the first team squad. Pre-season preparations are already underway, with trials and open sessions planned throughout the summer.
