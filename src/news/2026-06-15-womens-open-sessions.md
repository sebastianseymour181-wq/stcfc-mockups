The STCFC Women's section is hosting open training sessions at Shooting Field, Steyning from **Tuesday 17 June**.

All women are welcome, regardless of ability or experience. Sessions are a great way to meet the squad, try football in a friendly environment, and find out more about joining for the 2026–27 season.

**Details:**
- **When:** Starting Tuesday 17 June
- **Where:** Shooting Field, Steyning, BN44 3RQ
- **Who:** All women, all abilities

For more information, follow **@stcfcwomen** on Instagram or email steyningtownfc@gmail.com.
