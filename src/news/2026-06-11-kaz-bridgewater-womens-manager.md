Steyning Town Community FC are pleased to welcome back Kaz Bridgewater as Women's First Team Manager for the 2026–27 season.

Bridgewater returns to the club having previously managed the Women's side, bringing experience and continuity to the role. The Women's First Team compete in the London & South East Regional Women's Football League Division One South.

Pre-season open sessions have already begun, with the first session taking place on 17 June. All women are welcome to attend — no prior experience required.

For more information, follow **@stcfcwomen** on social media or email steyningtownfc@gmail.com.
